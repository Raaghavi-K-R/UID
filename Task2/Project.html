PROJECT

The Student Project Symposium is an event 
intended to showcase the type of projects 
that students conduct during their studies 
at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.
The event is held annually towards the end of 
Spring term at both the Chennai and 
Ettimadai campuses. It provides an opportunity
for students from the different programs 
to share their work, as well as get to 
see other projects.
The Symposium includes project posters, 
and demonstrations.Students, faculty, 
family members, employers, educational
and industrial partners, as well as 
other members of the public are 
invited to attend.
